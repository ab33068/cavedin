Caved In!

You and your lover are trapped in a cave due to a terrible hiking accident. To survive, you will have to gather resources in order to overcome hunger and thirst and build items to escape the dreary cave.

But beware...

Evil monsters are lurking in the shadows, waiting to feast upon you and your injured loved one. Will you be able to fend them off and escape the cave? Or will you succumb to the evil demons in the cave?
